Wanna work with us

There is always a place for players with a passion for work

What Will You Be Doing?

Create great content in English for a variety of marketing channels.

Creative Writer

Our products help developers and marketers around the world create cutting-edge websites. However, there are still some people that do not know about us yet. Your job is to craft content that will do the speaking for us, convincingly, naturally, and professionally.

Whether you are creating text for a simple landing page, social networking, or writing for our blog, you must take the concept and then adapt it for the target audience. You must also be able to work with both data and personas.

In this job, thorough preparation always precedes writing. And although mostly marketing topics await you, we also expect you to write more technical documents for developers. But don’t worry, you will have a strong team of marketing colleagues around you.

Who Are We Looking For?

A native (or near-native) English speaker that is a great writer and has fun writing.

Do you take to social networking like a fish to water? Great! You will also learn about online marketing and SEO. Plus, equally important is the ability to meet a topic you have never heard of before head on, find information about it, and then explain it simply. And if you have any work-related experience from the world of IT, climb onboard!
